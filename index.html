About Me

Thanks for stopping By!

Hello World! I am Nitya Kandukuri, a Web Developer living in sunny San Diego, California. I graduated with a Masters' Degree in Computer Engineering from the University of North Texas. I have had the oppurtunity to work as a Web Developer at the Electrical Engineering Department, Univeristy of North Texas.

The passion for web development happened by taking my favourite course, "Human Computer Interfaces". One thing led to another and I landed my first job as a part-time junior web developer at my university. These fortunate turn of events has sparked my inner interest towards Web development. After my graduation, I was offered a full-time web developer position at the Electrical Engineering department, UNT. I was actively involved from the start to finish of the architecture of the project with the amazing Central Web Maintenance Team at UNT.

Currenlty, I am enrolled in the UCSD Web Development Bootcamp Program where I am enhancing my skillset to master full stack web development.

In my free time, I love spending family time with my wonderful husband and the love of my life, my daughter. I also enjoy shopping, hiking, photography, drawing, and painting....Go Figure!
